## 🌍 About Transgate

Transgate is a **speech-to-text AI tool** that helps individuals and teams **transform spoken content into structured knowledge**.  
Built for **accuracy, privacy, and flexibility**, Transgate supports **over 50 languages** and allows you to **summarize**, **analyze**, and even **chat with your transcripts**.

We believe that every recording has untapped value — Transgate helps you unlock it.

## 🤖 AI-Powered Capabilities

Transgate integrates advanced **Large Language Models (LLMs)** for intelligent transcript understanding:

- **Summarization & Highlights:** Extracts core ideas, topics, and keywords automatically.  
- **Smart Q&A:** Ask questions directly about your recording.  
- **Action Item Extraction:** Detects next steps, to-dos, and key insights.  
- **Topic Segmentation:** Groups transcript parts by theme.  

These AI features help teams **save hours** and turn raw speech into actionable data.
